Mountain Lion

Mountain lions are the fourth largest cat species in the world and the largest cat that purr.

  • Scientific Name: Puma Concolor
  • Average Length: 6 to 8 Feet
  • Average Lifespan: 8 to 13 Years
  • Habitat: Mountainous Terrain

The mountain lion, scientifically known as Puma concolor, is a large cat species found in various habitats across the Americas. Known for its agility and strength, it is a solitary predator that primarily preys on deer and other mammals. Mountain lions are adaptable creatures, able to thrive in diverse environments from forests to deserts.
